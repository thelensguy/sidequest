# Privacy Policy — SideQuest

_Last updated: July 14, 2026_

SideQuest is a Chrome extension that helps you track job applications. This page explains what data it handles and how.

## What SideQuest stores

- Job application details you save or type in (company, role, link, status, dates)
- A log of your activity within the extension (captures, status changes), used to compute XP, levels, and badges
- Your reward-wheel treat list and a few interface preferences (theme, capture-bubble visibility)

## Where that data lives

Entirely on your own device, in Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.local`. SideQuest has no backend server, no analytics, no third-party tracking, and no account system. Nothing you enter is ever transmitted anywhere — not to the developer, not to any third party.

## What SideQuest reads from web pages

On LinkedIn, Indeed, and ZipRecruiter job-posting pages, SideQuest reads the visible job title, company name, and URL from the page so you can save them with one click via the floating capture button. It does not read anything else on those pages, does not track your browsing, and takes no action on any other site unless you manually open the toolbar popup yourself.

## Data export and deletion

You can export everything SideQuest has stored as a JSON file at any time (Options → Data), delete any individual entry from the dashboard, or remove all of it by uninstalling the extension — Chrome clears `chrome.storage.local` for an extension when it's removed.
